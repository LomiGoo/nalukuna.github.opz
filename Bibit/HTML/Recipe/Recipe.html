Adobo
  • Chicken or pork
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Bay leaves
  1. Marinate the meat in soy sauce and garlic.
  2. Cook the marinated meat in a pan.
  3. Add vinegar, pepper, and bay leaves.
  4. Simmer until tender.
Sinigang
  • Pork or shrimp
  • Tamarind mix
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Water spinach (Kangkong)
  • Eggplant
  1. Boil the meat with onions and tomatoes.
  2. Add tamarind mix and vegetables.
  3. Simmer until the meat and vegetables are tender.
Spaghetti
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Sauté garlic and onions.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  5. Mix the sauce with the pasta and top with cheese.
Maja Blanca
  • Cornstarch
  • Coconut milk
  • Corn kernels
  • Condensed milk
  • Sugar
  1. Mix cornstarch, coconut milk, and sugar in a pot.
  2. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly.
  3. Add corn kernels and condensed milk.
  4. Pour into a mold and let it set.
Pancit
  • Rice noodles
  • Chicken or pork
  • Carrots
  • Cabbage
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  1. Soak the noodles in water.
  2. Sauté garlic and meat in a pan.
  3. Add vegetables and soy sauce.
  4. Mix the noodles with the sautéed ingredients.
